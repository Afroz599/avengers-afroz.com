Robert Downey, Jr. (born April 4, 1965, New York, New York, U.S.) is one of Hollywood’s most gifted and versatile actors.
He earned acclaim for such early films as Chaplin (1992) and later found considerable fame as the superhero Iron Man in the Marvel film franchise.
In 2024 Downey won the Academy Award for best supporting actor for his performance in Oppenheimer (2023).
